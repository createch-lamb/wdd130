Utah

In the winter, you can ski and snowboard and in the rest of the seasons you can hike at the majestic mountains that surrounds you. There is a hike for anyone! There are plenty of places to boat and kayak. In Utah, we love our family. Any time is family time. States that are close by are Colorado, Arizona, Las Vegas, Idaho and Wyoming.
